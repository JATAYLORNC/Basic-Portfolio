About Me

Jeff Taylor has most recently been a student in the Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, an intensive 24-week long program dedicated to designing and building web applications. At UNC, Jeff has developed and continues to hone his skills in HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars.js, React.js, C#, and Asp.net.

Prior to that, Jeff held Senior Applications Engineering positions at both Lenovo and IBM. For the past 3 years at Lenovo, Jeff led a global team of engineers, directing all technical procurement activities associated with the design, development, testing, and sourcing of printed circuit boards. At IBM, Jeff developed and mastered a deep understanding of printed circuit boards in a career long learning process culminating in his position as the lead subject matter expert at IBM.
